Refund Policy

Our guidelines for refunds and cancellations.

1. Introduction

At MarineMonks ("we," "our," or "us"), we strive to ensure your complete satisfaction with our services. This Refund Policy outlines our procedures and guidelines regarding refunds and cancellations for our products and services.

By purchasing any product or service from MarineMonks, you agree to the terms of this Refund Policy. Please read this policy carefully before making any purchases.

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. Your continued use of our services after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Refund Policy.

2. Subscription Plans

2.1 Cancellation of Subscription

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting our customer support team. Upon cancellation:

  • You will continue to have access to your subscription benefits until the end of your current billing period.
  • Your subscription will not automatically renew for the next billing period.
  • No partial refunds will be provided for the unused portion of your current billing period unless otherwise specified in this policy.
  • Any downloadable content that you have already accessed or downloaded will remain accessible to you even after cancellation.

2.2 Monthly Subscriptions

For monthly subscription plans:

  • You may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial purchase if you have not accessed more than 20% of the course content.
  • After the first 7 days, or if you have accessed more than 20% of the course content, no refunds will be provided for the current billing period.
  • If you encounter technical issues that significantly impair your ability to access our services, you may be eligible for a partial or full refund at our discretion.
  • To request a refund for a monthly subscription, you must contact our customer support team within the specified timeframe and provide details about your subscription and the reason for your refund request.

2.3 Annual Subscriptions

For annual subscription plans:

  • You may request a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase if you have not accessed more than 10% of the course content.
  • After the first 14 days but within the first 30 days, if you have accessed less than 20% of the course content, you may be eligible for a partial refund of 70% of the subscription fee.
  • Between 31 and 60 days from the purchase date, if you have accessed less than 30% of the course content, you may be eligible for a partial refund of 50% of the subscription fee.
  • After 60 days, no refunds will be provided for annual subscriptions.
  • To request a refund for an annual subscription, you must contact our customer support team within the specified timeframe and provide details about your subscription and the reason for your refund request.

2.4 Multi-Year Subscriptions

For multi-year subscription plans:

  • You may request a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase if you have not accessed more than 10% of the course content.
  • After the first 14 days but within the first 30 days, if you have accessed less than 20% of the course content, you may be eligible for a partial refund of 80% of the subscription fee.
  • Between 31 and 90 days from the purchase date, if you have accessed less than 30% of the course content, you may be eligible for a partial refund of 60% of the subscription fee.
  • After 90 days, no refunds will be provided for multi-year subscriptions.
  • To request a refund for a multi-year subscription, you must contact our customer support team within the specified timeframe and provide details about your subscription and the reason for your refund request.

3. One-Time Purchases

3.1 Study Materials and Mock Tests

For one-time purchases of study materials and mock tests:

  • You may request a full refund within 48 hours of purchase if you have not downloaded or accessed the materials.
  • Once you have downloaded or accessed the materials, no refunds will be provided due to the digital nature of these products.
  • If the materials contain significant errors or inaccuracies that substantially diminish their value, you may be eligible for a partial or full refund at our discretion.
  • To request a refund for study materials or mock tests, you must contact our customer support team within the specified timeframe and provide details about your purchase and the reason for your refund request.

3.2 Live Sessions and Webinars

For live sessions and webinars:

  • You may request a full refund up to 72 hours before the scheduled start time.
  • For cancellations between 72 hours and 24 hours before the scheduled start time, a 50% refund will be provided.
  • No refunds will be provided for cancellations less than 24 hours before the scheduled start time.
  • If you miss a live session, you will have access to the recording (if available) but will not be eligible for a refund.
  • If a live session or webinar is canceled or rescheduled by MarineMonks, you will be offered the option to attend the rescheduled session or receive a full refund.
  • To request a refund for a live session or webinar, you must contact our customer support team within the specified timeframe and provide details about your purchase and the reason for your refund request.

3.3 Bundled Packages

For bundled packages that include multiple products or services:

  • You may request a full refund within 7 days of purchase if you have not accessed more than 10% of the bundled content.
  • After 7 days or if you have accessed more than 10% of the bundled content, no refunds will be provided for bundled packages.
  • Partial refunds for individual components of a bundled package are not available.
  • To request a refund for a bundled package, you must contact our customer support team within the specified timeframe and provide details about your purchase and the reason for your refund request.

4. Technical Issues

If you experience technical issues that significantly impair your ability to access or use our services:

  • You must report the issue to our customer support team within 48 hours of encountering the problem.
  • Our team will work diligently to resolve the issue in a timely manner.
  • You must cooperate with our technical support team and provide any information requested to help diagnose and resolve the issue.
  • If we are unable to resolve the issue within a reasonable timeframe (typically 5 business days), you may be eligible for a partial or full refund, determined on a case-by-case basis.
  • Technical issues caused by your own hardware, software, or internet connection are not eligible for refunds.
  • To request a refund due to technical issues, you must provide detailed information about the problem, including screenshots or error messages if available.

5. Special Circumstances

We understand that special circumstances may arise. In the following cases, you may be eligible for a refund outside our standard policy:

5.1 Medical Emergencies

If you experience a serious medical emergency that prevents you from using our services:

  • You must provide documentation from a medical professional within 30 days of the emergency.
  • Depending on the circumstances, we may offer a full refund, partial refund, or extend your subscription period.
  • Each case will be evaluated individually.

5.2 Natural Disasters

If you are affected by a natural disaster that prevents you from using our services:

  • You must contact our customer support team within 30 days of the event.
  • We may request documentation or verification of the impact of the disaster.
  • Depending on the circumstances, we may offer a full refund, partial refund, or extend your subscription period.

5.3 Military Deployment

If you are an active military personnel who is deployed:

  • You must provide a copy of your deployment orders within 30 days of receiving them.
  • We may offer a full refund, partial refund, or suspend your subscription until your return.
  • Each case will be evaluated individually.

5.4 Death or Incapacitation

In the unfortunate event of death or incapacitation of a subscriber:

  • A family member or legal representative must contact our customer support team and provide appropriate documentation.
  • We will provide a pro-rated refund for the unused portion of the subscription.

All special circumstances will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis, and we reserve the right to request documentation to verify your situation.

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Log in to your MarineMonks account.
  2. Navigate to the "Billing" or "Payment History" section.
  3. Select the purchase for which you want to request a refund.
  4. Click on the "Request Refund" button and follow the instructions.

Alternatively, you can contact our customer support team at refunds@marinemonks.in with the following information:

  • Your full name and email address associated with your account
  • Date of purchase
  • Order or transaction ID (if available)
  • Product or service purchased
  • Reason for requesting a refund
  • Any relevant documentation (if applicable)

We will acknowledge receipt of your refund request within 2 business days and process it within 5-7 business days.

7. Refund Processing

Once your refund request is approved:

  • Refunds will be processed using the original payment method used for the purchase.
  • Processing times may vary depending on your payment provider:
    • Credit/debit card refunds: 5-10 business days
    • PayPal refunds: 3-5 business days
    • Bank transfers: 7-14 business days
  • For credit card payments, refunds may appear as pending on your account before being fully processed by your bank or credit card company.
  • If the original payment method is no longer available or valid, we will work with you to determine an alternative refund method.
  • Please note that we are not responsible for any fees charged by your financial institution in connection with the refund.

8. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are non-refundable under any circumstances:

  • Administrative fees
  • Transaction fees charged by payment processors
  • Promotional or discounted purchases marked as "non-refundable"
  • Services that have been fully delivered or completed
  • Downloadable content that has been accessed or downloaded
  • Add-on services or products purchased separately from a subscription
  • Subscription time that has already elapsed

Additionally, we do not provide refunds for purchases made through third-party resellers or platforms. For such purchases, you must contact the third party directly regarding their refund policies.

9. Promotional Credits and Coupons

For purchases made using promotional credits, coupons, or discount codes:

  • Refunds will be issued in the same form as the original payment.
  • If a purchase was made using a combination of promotional credits and other payment methods, only the portion paid by other methods will be refunded.
  • Promotional credits issued as part of a refund may have different expiration dates or terms than the original credits.
  • Expired promotional credits or coupons cannot be reactivated or refunded.

10. Abuse of Refund Policy

We reserve the right to refuse refund requests if we detect a pattern of abuse, including but not limited to:

  • Multiple refund requests from the same user
  • Suspicious activity indicating an attempt to defraud our refund system
  • Violation of our Terms of Service
  • Evidence of content piracy or unauthorized sharing
  • Refund requests that appear to be made in bad faith

If we determine that a user is abusing our refund policy, we may:

  • Deny current and future refund requests
  • Suspend or terminate the user's account
  • Take legal action if necessary

11. Exchanges and Account Credits

In some cases, instead of a refund, we may offer:

  • An exchange for a different product or service of equal value
  • Account credit that can be used for future purchases
  • An extension of your subscription period

These alternatives may be offered at our discretion or upon your request. Account credits typically do not expire and can be used for any purchase on our platform.

12. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. Your continued use of our services after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Refund Policy.

For refund requests, the Refund Policy in effect at the time of purchase will apply. However, if the new policy is more favorable to you, we may apply the new policy at our discretion.
