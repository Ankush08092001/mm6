Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans after subscribing?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. If you downgrade, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

How does the billing cycle work?

For monthly plans, you'll be billed every 30 days from the date of your initial subscription. For annual plans, you'll be billed once a year from your subscription date. All plans auto-renew unless cancelled.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and RuPay. We also support UPI payments and net banking for Indian customers.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you cancel, you'll still have access to your plan until the end of your current billing period. We don't offer partial refunds for unused time, except during the 7-day satisfaction guarantee period.

Do you offer discounts for groups or institutions?

Yes, we offer special pricing for maritime academies, training institutes, and groups of 5 or more students. Please contact our sales team at sales@marinemonks.com for more information on institutional pricing.
